# Changeable Pricing Section

A polished pricing selector designed for modern SaaS onboarding and checkout experiences. It combines a spring-animated billing toggle with expandable plan details to help users compare plans quickly and confidently.

- **Animated Billing Toggle**: Smooth spring indicator between monthly and yearly.
- **Selectable Plans**: Radio-style selection with tactile feedback.
- **Expandable Features**: Selected plan reveals detailed feature list.
- **Blur + Slide Pricing Motion**: Price transitions feel fast and premium.
- **Theme Adaptive**: Carefully tuned for light and dark environments.
