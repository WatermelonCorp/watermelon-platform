# Lead Dashboard

Features:

- Interactive Kanban board for tracking leads across different stages.
- Drag-and-drop functionality for moving lead cards between columns.
- Dialogs for creating new leads and adding cards to specific columns.
- Filtering options to view leads by status, assignee, and time.
- Overview of total and closed leads.
